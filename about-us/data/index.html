Data

The data we use

We rely primarily on data for street networks from Open Street Map (OSM) and for population densities from worldpop. Additional data used to calibrate global-scale models are obtained from the Integrated Public Use Microdata Series, hosted by the Minnesota Population Center at Minnesota University, U.S.A.
